Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how GrocMed handles your information.

Effective Date: May 2026

Welcome to GrocMed. We are committed to protecting your personal information and maintaining transparency in how your data is handled while using our platform.

Information We Collect

  • Name and account details
  • Email address and phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Order and purchase history
  • Basic device and app usage information

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and manage orders
  • Improve user experience and app functionality
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order updates and important service notifications

Permissions

GrocMed only requests permissions necessary for app functionality such as internet access and notifications.

We do not intentionally access camera, microphone, contacts, or location unless future features explicitly require user permission.

Third-Party Services

We may work with trusted third-party providers for payment processing, analytics, notifications, and delivery support.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Children's Privacy

GrocMed is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
